No Signal

Several countries still have little to no internet access.
Either due to poor economic conditions, geographical hinderances, or weak satellite signal.

Connection

Lien Gold is attempting to solve that problem with a fleet of quadcopters. They would provide a wireless network between each other and individuals on the ground enabling the ability to finally connect online with their family and friends.
